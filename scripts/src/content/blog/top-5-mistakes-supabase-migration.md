Migrating a database is risky. Here are the top mistakes we see teams make:

1. **Forgetting RLS Policies**: Leaving tables open to the public.
2. **Ignoring Sequences**: Not updating primary key sequences, causing duplicate key errors.
3. **Downtime Underestimation**: Assuming a massive data dump will be fast.
